What is Cokora?

Cokora is single player, story based puzzle game. The goal of the game is to traverse the level by platforming, breaking and moving objects. The puzzles are designed with emergent gameplay in mind so that the player can traverse in different ways. This was a 4 week long school project I was apart of, together with 11 other people. The team consisted of 3 programmers, 3 game designers, 6 artists.
