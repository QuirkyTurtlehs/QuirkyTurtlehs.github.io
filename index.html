Engine: Unity 2020

Development time: 7 weeks

Team Size: 10

Safety Protocol is a narrative driven, environmental puzzle game that lets you explore an eerie space station while chatting with the stations AI. The main mechanic of the game is the interactive power system. Hidden fuse boxes can be reactivated to increase your power supply which can then be used to power different areas inside the station. This is used to unlock new paths, letting you delve deeper into the story.

Engine: Unity 2018

Development time: 7 weeks

Dinozards is a couch-multiplayer arena game where up to four players battle each other free-for-all style. Players have three abilities at their disposal, a fireball, a reflect ability and a dash. Using them and position yourself favorably is the key to victory as the last player standing wins. The game also has a 2v2 mode for epic team battles.
