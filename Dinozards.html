What is Dinozards?

Dinozards is a multiplayer arena game where up to four players battle each other free-for-all style. Players have three abilities at their disposal, a fireball, a reflect ability and a dash. Using them and position yourself favorably is the key to victory as the last player standing wins. The game also has a 2v2 mode for epic team battles.
